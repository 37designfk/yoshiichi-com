Tradition and Innovation

With the eye for quality passed down through generations in Akashi,
we hand-pick seafood of the highest quality.
Through the introduction of advanced freshness management systems,
We answer the trust of our customers.

Commitment to Quality

With Akashi's traditional cooking methods
we pursue the fusion of new food cultures.
Adopting the latest hygiene management technologies,
we deliver safe and delicious products.

Coexistence with the Region

We treasure strong bonds with local fishermen
and contribute to the development of the local fisheries industry.
We pass on the food culture of Akashi to the next generation
and contribute to regional revitalization.
